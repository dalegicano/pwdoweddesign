The Philippine Web Designers Organization is a volunteer non-profit that helps nurture the local design & development community.

We are a group of enthusiasts and professionals who create human interfaces for the web, champion the use of standards, accessibility, & usability, and aim to uplift the state of web design in the country.

We organize events like the form function() & .class conference, as well as meetups, roadshows, seminars, workshops, and hackathons.
